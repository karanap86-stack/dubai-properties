### 1. Intent-Based Property Scoring

### 3. Government Integration Layer
**Single-Window Clearance:**
- 28 state property registration portals (API integration)
- Digital slot booking + document upload
- Online stamp duty payment (govt receives directly)
- Real-time status tracking
- Same-day deed download
- **Result:** Registration 14 days ‚Üí 1 day (14x faster)

### 4. Document Template Library (130+ Templates)
**Eliminates Google Docs/WhatsApp:**
- Rental agreements, sale deeds, POA, gift deeds (state-specific)
- Auto-fill from platform database
- E-signature integration (DocuSign/Aadhaar)
- Version control + audit trail
- **Result:** Zero external platforms needed

### 5. State-Based RERA Filtering
**Critical for India's 28-State Complexity:**
- Partners upload RERA licenses (1 or 10 states)
- Notifications only for licensed states (100% relevance)
- Same ‚Çπ10K/month for 1 state or 10 states
- **Result:** Pan-India reach at zero extra cost

### 6. Bring-Your-Own-Vendor
**Relationship Preservation:**
- Partners add existing service providers (10-year lawyer stays)
- Platform invites vendors (free 3 months if referred)
- Auto-assignment to partner's deals
- Platform routes payments (takes 2-5%)
- **Result:** Partners adopt without losing relationships

### 7. AI Auto-Learning System
**Cost Optimization Over Time:**
- 4-tier quality system (AI only ‚Üí AI + human)
- Learns from corrections daily
- Month 0: 80% Tier 1 (automatic) ‚Üí Month 24: 95%
- Cost per photo: ‚Çπ112 ‚Üí ‚Çπ53.50 (52% reduction)
- **Result:** Margins improve 12% over 24 months

### 8. Infrastructure Timeline Tracking
**Government Accountability:**
- Real-time tracking of metro, roads, drainage projects
- Property impact analysis (post-metro: +25% appreciation)
- Delay alerts (Metro Line 3 delayed by 12 months)
- Budget tracking (‚Çπ50 Cr drainage project shows govt commitment)
- **Result:** Informed investment decisions, govt accountability
