# In-House Real Estate CRM

A modern, scalable CRM for real estate, with full omnichannel, campaign, and department/role-based access control. Includes:
- Lead, contact, deal, and task management
- Departmental hierarchy (HR, Sales, Accounts, Marketing, Support, Tech, etc.)
- Master Admin, backup admin, department admins, managers, agents (human & AI)
- Telephony, WhatsApp, and email integration points
- Campaign automation and targeting
- Audit trail and reporting
- API endpoints for external CRM sync
- Secure, future-proof, and extensible
